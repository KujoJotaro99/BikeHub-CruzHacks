    • Our Community

    Our mission is to connect bikers across the world and provide a powerful platform to share your rides! Biker Buddy allows you to create your own profile and participate in location based biker hubs. Your profile is your portal to finding new friends and creating memories on the road

    Want to know how long you rode for? The hubs and popular routes you covered? Health analytics? It's all taken care of! Enabling our live track feature before rides allows you to map your journey and recieve a report about your ride. You can even track changes to your route habits and performance history by accessing ride records. Want to say something in the community or discuss important events? Share your journey and stay up to date within the biker community through our share feature!
